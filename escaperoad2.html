Escape Road 2

Escape Road is back with Escape Road 2! Get ready for the thrilling sequel that takes you on heart-pounding chasing and escaping gameplay! Your mission? Navigate the crowded roads, escape from the police, and survive! It's now updated with more vehicles, upgrades, and environments!

What is Escape Road 2

Escape Road 2 is the second version of the famous Escape Road game that brings players to an endless chase from police officers. However, this chase will be much more intense and dramatic than the original version. The police have been improved to be faster and smarter to chase you. They are more determined, have better weapons, and employ more sophisticated tactics. It's a driving game that puts players behind the wheel in a thrilling race against the police. Master high-speed maneuvers, outsmart relentless chasers, unlock new powerful vehicles, and escape!

How to play the Escape Road 2 game

Drive, smash, escape

As you may anticipate from a thrilling chase game, the rules of Escape Road 2 are extremely straightforward but challenging. Your main goal is to stay out of trouble with the law for as long as you can. Your pursuit will be relentless, and the power of the police cars and military helicopters will only increase.

All you must do is to drive your car at maximum speed while avoiding objects like cars and buildings. When your automobile reaches its maximum energy, it can even destroy other cars. The objective is to survive as long as you can. If the police vehicles get you, the game is over. Race against the police and take a spot on the leaderboard. 

Unlock new vehicles

Along the race, you should collect and accumulate coins. Then, you can use them to unlock a lot of amazing new cars in the shop. From Common to Rare to Epic to Legendary, there are up to 90 new cars that are just waiting for you to unlock. There are also armored tanks, race cars, luxury supercars, and much more.

New features added

Compared to the original version, Escape Road 2 game has been improved with many new features, promising to bring players a better experience. This game is equipped with more modern new cars along with more attractive new graphics. There are also many vehicle customization options present. Players can also get their hands on various environments. 

What's more fantastic?

In this part, players can get out of the car if it's stuck and find a new one! And when your vehicle explodes, you will be ejected from the vehicle with a parachute and land on the ground. After that, you can find a new vehicle and escape. So look around and hop on any vehicle that you find on the street and start racing against the police.

There are even more impressive features that we recommend you to find out yourself! 

Game Controls

To start and turn: Use A/D or left/right arrows

To drive: Use A/S/D or Left/Down/Right arrows.

To enter or exit a vehicle: Press the Spacebar.

Police cars will be pursuing you as soon as your automobile leaves the bank. The Arrow keys can be used to control the car's direction. There are numerous roads and automobiles in this city.

That's what's obstructing your escape path. Keep an eye out and move quickly to dodge all the cars on the road.

Since your car will stop if you collide with another vehicle. The police car will come racing in and encircle you to take you into custody right away. So, be careful and clever!

Tips and strategies to survive the police chase

Avoid driving in congested areas as much as possible. There will be many challenges and obstacles in this busy city.

Buy upgrades to increase your car's power as well as test out different driving experiences.

Make abrupt turns instead of driving straight to divert the attention of the police. Also, it will help you avoid dead ends.
